About Us | Krunker-game.github.io

Last Updated: May 27, 2025

Krunker-game.github.io is an independent web platform focused on delivering free and accessible HTML5 games to users around the world. We offer a browser-based experience requiring no downloads, logins, or subscriptions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide simple, fun, and instant access to quality games for everyone—whether you're at home, in school, or just passing time. The site is designed to be fast, lightweight, and compatible with all modern devices.

What We Provide

  • A curated selection of HTML5 games available for free play
  • Mobile and desktop-friendly interfaces
  • Advertising-supported access powered by Google AdSense
  • Analytics-based improvements using Google Analytics

User Engagement

We allow users to interact via comments and encourage constructive and respectful participation. Comments are moderated to maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all age groups.

Advertising and Transparency

This site operates with advertising to support its maintenance and growth. We believe in transparency and provide clear information through our Privacy Policy, Terms, and DMCA guidelines.

Legal Information

All game content remains the intellectual property of their respective creators. If you believe your content is used improperly, please refer to our DMCA section for resolution steps.

Contact

We welcome your feedback, suggestions, and questions. Reach out through the method listed in the footer of our website. Thank you for being a part of our gaming community!
